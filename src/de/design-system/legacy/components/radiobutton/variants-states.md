## Zustände
Die Komponente hat folgende Zustände: {.lead}
* Checked {.lead}
* Unchecked {.lead}
* Focused checked {.lead}
* Focused unchecked {.lead}
* Disabled checked {.lead}
* Disabled unchecked {.lead}
