## What does the pattern do?
* Gives live feedback on how well written the prompt is.  

## When should the pattern be used?
* Can help (especially beginners) to write better prompts.

## Recommendation
* Depending on the context, the quality of the prompt may depend on other dimensions. For example, length, details, specificity or contextual description.
